Paid vs Non-paid

Paid = orders/revenue from Amazon Ads + Meta Ads. Non-paid = total − paid, attributed to Klaviyo (email) and Organic (direct, search, seeding). ROAS = paid revenue ÷ ad spend.

Strict month-to-date

Only posts dated within the current month count. The trackers carry real post dates; TikTok crawl dates decode from the post ID. Stale/undated items are excluded — no padded history.

Data feeds & refresh

Trackers are pulled live from Google Sheets (you update by 10 AM); sales is your local data/sales.csv; the report rebuilds and delivers daily at 1 PM ICT. Tracker overlaps are de-duplicated by URL.
