04 · The Activity — Cô Ba's Fortune Station

Draw a quẻ, keep it, post it — aloe is the reward

Xin xăm, the Vietnamese temple fortune ritual. Every guest keeps their quẻ — and the ones who share it become our content.

SHAKE

the rattan cup of numbered fortune sticks

DRAW & KEEP

your stick matches a printed quẻ — a keepsake fortune card, yours to take

SNAP & TAG

photo with your quẻ at the lantern corner → post to IG, tag @cobasdaughter

RECEIVE ALOE

show the post, take home an Aloe Gel — the recovery your fortune promised

Every quẻ also carries the voucher QR (email signup → discount code) — no post required. Aloe gifts capped per day; one per guest; stock sized by Head of Products.
